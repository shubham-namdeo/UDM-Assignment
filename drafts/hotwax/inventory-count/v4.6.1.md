## What changed (in plain English)

## User impact
Users will now experience a cleaner and more accurate interface when managing their scan history. Removing a scan no longer leaves confusing empty spaces, ensuring the list remains readable and easy to navigate during an active inventory count.

## Operational impact
This fix eliminates visual clutter that could lead to confusion during the audit process. Warehouse staff can confidently adjust their scans without the risk of misinterpreting blank rows as missing data or system errors.

## Business impact
By maintaining a clear and accurate UI during inventory counts, this update improves operational efficiency and data integrity. It reduces the time spent on manual verification and ensures that inventory records reflect actual scan actions without technical distractions.
