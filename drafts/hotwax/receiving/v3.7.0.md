# receiving – Release v3.7.0

## What changed (in plain English)
- You will now receive automatic notifications for new transfer orders, ensuring you're always in the loop and ready to act {#643}.

## User impact
You’ll get immediate alerts when new transfer orders are created, allowing you to respond faster and reduce manual checking. This keeps you better informed and focused on processing tasks.

## Operational impact
This update streamlines your workflow by reducing delays in acknowledging and processing new transfers. It enhances team coordination, ensuring everyone is promptly aware of incoming orders.

## Business impact
By accelerating the receiving process, this feature helps improve inventory accuracy and turnover. It contributes to overall efficiency and responsiveness, enabling your business to meet demand more effectively.
